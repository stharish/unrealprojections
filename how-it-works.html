How It Works

1. Choose a Facade Pack

Select Christmas or Halloween, with options from 5 to 20 facades.

2. Map Your House with LumaLux (Free)

Included with every pack is 30-day access to LumaLux to create your outline.

3. Send Us Your Outline

We match the facades perfectly to your home’s geometry.

4. Receive Your Custom Video

Your show is delivered in 1080p/4K and ready to play on any projector.
